# 🚀 Interview Assist: User Quick-Start Guide

Welcome to the future of objective, data-driven hiring. **Interview Assist** is an AI-native co-pilot designed to eliminate recruiter bias and synthesize deep technical signals from raw interview transcripts and resumes.

---

## 🏗️ Step 1: Establish Your Role (The JD)
Every successful hire starts with a clear yardstick. 
1. **Create a Role**: Click **"Add JD"** on the dashboard. 
2. **Define Rubrics**: The system uses specialized rubrics (e.g., *Frontend Mastery*, *Leadership*, *Culture Fit*) based on the role category. 
3. **View Master JD**: Use the **"View Job Description"** button to see a beautifully formatted, AI-parsed version of your requirements.

## 📂 Step 2: Source & Audit (The Resume)
Add candidates to your pipeline with zero manual data entry.
1. **Upload Resume**: Select a `.pdf` or `.docx` resume in the **"Add Candidate"** slot.
2. **Instant Scoring**: Our AI audits the resume against your specific role rubrics and gives a **0-100 Resume Fit Score**.
3. **Executive Summary**: Read the "Executive Summary" to understand *why* the AI assigned the score, highlighting strengths and missing gaps.

## 🎙️ Step 3: Prepare Your Squad (The Guide)
Ensure every interviewer asks the right questions.
1. **Set Stage to "Interview"**: Once a candidate is ready, move them to the "Interview" status.
2. **Generate Guide**: Open the **"Interview Guide"** tab. The AI will cross-reference the candidate's resume against the JD to find critical "Gaps" (e.g., *"Resume mentions React but lacks details on state management"*).
3. **Standardized Questions**: Share the generated questions and **"What to look for"** signals with your interviewers to ensure consistency.

## 🧠 Step 4: Evidence-Based Verdicts (The Feedback)
Stop relying on "vibes." Use facts.
1. **Upload Transcript**: After the call, upload the raw **Interview Transcript** (or a rough draft) into the **"Interview Analysis"** cockpit.
2. **Interviewer Notes**: Add your own notes. These are weighted at **70%**—the AI will prioritize your observations over the raw text but will flag a **"Subjectivity Warning"** if your notes conflict with the transcript evidence.
3. **Process Round**: Watch the AI audit the conversation, extract quotes as proof, and assign a normalized score for that specific round.

## 📈 Step 5: The Holistic Verdict (Cumulative Analysis)
As the candidate progresses through multiple rounds, the system synthesizes everything.
- **Portfolio Summary**: A single, aggregated narrative that tracks the candidate's growth and helps you make the final "Hire" or "No Hire" decision.
- **Decision Confidence**: The AI calculates an aggregate score across all rounds, highlighting consistency or outlier performance.

---

### 💡 Pro-Tips for Power Users:
- **Be Specific in Notes**: If you note a technical concern in your manual notes, the AI "Hiring Committee Lead" will prioritize that observation during the audit.
- **Collated Evidence**: Use the "Analysis" row headers to jump to specific evidence (quotes) found by the AI in the transcript.
- **Reverse Chronology**: Your latest interview analysis always sits at the top for quick access.
